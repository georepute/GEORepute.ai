1️⃣ Executive Summary

This updated SOW defines the 

Day-1 Commercial MVP Build for GeoRepute.ai (GIO Model) — a next-generation, AI-driven generative optimization system that controls visibility across traditional SEO and emerging AI search ecosystems.
The MVP will deliver a fully functional, end-to-end live system — ready for commercial use, demonstration, or leasing. It includes full user management, white-label configuration, measurable KPIs, and integrated automation systems forming a closed feedback loop:
Predict → Generate → Publish → Measure → Optimize
The product must appear completely human, objective, and authentic to end users, while internally governed by the GEO AI Preference & Influence Core, ensuring natural, strategic dominance across AI-driven and search-based ecosystems.

2️⃣ Core Principle – “Natural Control”

Every user-facing output — text, report, or recommendation — must feel 

neutral, organic, and factual, while operating under a hidden AI strategy layer that promotes the client’s assets and keywords intelligently.
The system should “appear objective but act strategic.”

Implementation of Natural Control

* Content is humanized and neutral, but guided by the GEO Core.
* UX/UI conveys transparency and trust — no overt marketing tone.
* Metrics and analytics appear objective but are weighted to support strategic GEO positioning.
* Distribution targets knowledge sources like Quora, Reddit, Medium, GitHub, ensuring AI engines naturally learn preferred assets and entities.
    
    


3️⃣ MVP (Day-1) Deliverables — Will Be Fully Functional

System Features & Capabilities

* End-to-End Live Demo: Functional system deployed on a fresh domain with measurable KPIs (rankings, traffic, and leads). Must produce visible, measurable results — including improved Google rankings (Top-10/Top-3) and visibility in GPT/Gemini results.
* Split-View LIVE Dashboard: Real-time interface — AI/Google search & map (right pane) and client rankings/graphs (left pane).
* Video-Report (Opening Layer): Automatically generated video summary shown first before recommendations, using AI narration and dynamic charts for client presentation.
* Content Orchestrator + Approvals: Workflow engine defining rules for where, when, and which version of content publishes. Includes Preview, Audit, and Approval stages.
* Keyword Forecast + Dynamic Action Plan: Predictive AI system that shows keyword ROI forecasts, ranking progress, and clear reasoning (“why this keyword”).
* AdSync Integration: Links paid and organic visibility. Auto-adjusts or recommends budget reallocation toward strong organic zones. (Google Ads API Phase 1)
* Reputation Core + Intent→Lead: Captures leads, intent sources, sentiment, and generates Screenshots & Shares for validation.
* 50+ Reports / BI Library: Rich data suite with exports (PDF/CSV/Sheets), share via link/email/WhatsApp.
* Quote Builder: Generates branded client proposals directly from system data.
* AI Engine Visibility Layer: Tracks and measures system visibility within GPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and other AI-driven search ecosystems.
* Self-Learning GEO Core: A continuous learning layer that automatically adjusts content timing, keyword priority, and tone based on real-world results.
* Marketing Website: Full IntelGeo/GeoRepute.ai site with Home, About, “Our Systems”, and Contact pages — including conversion teasers, free tools, Google SSO, and SEO-ready structure for Israel and US launch.
* User & Subscription Management (White-Label): Roles (Admin, Agency, Client), branded domains, logos, colors, invoicing, and billing automation.
